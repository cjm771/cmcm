CMCM is a simple sexy content manager for designers, built with PHP and Javascript, and a database-less storage tactic. That means you can download, drag the folders to your server, and your ready to go!

Frunt is the Front end counterpart to CMCM, and is included. It is a toolkit for developers and template makers to quickly develop dope boy websites. Frunt includes core access methods to your CMCM files, but also useful widgets like menus, slideshows, scrollers, modals, and more. Consider it the bootstrap for cmcm.
